Projects

Rural Mobility and Access

During the summer of 2024 and the spring of 2025, I worked as a research assistant on a CTS seed grant project entitled Rural Mobility and Access: Leveraging Big Data Analytics and Context-Aware Computing, supervised by Dr. Ying Song and Dr. Di Zhu. By integrating geographic contexts into the analysis of mobile phone data and the interpretation of mobility and accessibility, our project addresses the inadequate data for rural areas and supports future projects on urban transportation developments.

We use R-Shiny to develop interactive pages for local transportation planners, so they are able to fetch the mobility flow patterns flexibly.

Our work, entitled A Behavior-Informed and Geo-Context-Aware Home Detection Framework for Mobile Phone Positioning Data , has also been presented at the 33rd ACM International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems (SIGSPATIAL’25) and the 2025 CTS Transportation Research Conference in November 2025.
